Functions of Our tracker

Click on the buttons to know what this product does:

Add income

At the beginning of the month,add your income amount once in our tracker and it will use it to monitor you income and ratio percentage.

Add expenses

You can add all your expenses and allot them in a suitable category,Our tracker will plot them graphically and show how much was spent in each category.

According to the expenses carried out in the month,our tracker will subtract it from your income and tell how much money is remaining to be spent.Accordingly,the user can plan their expense.
